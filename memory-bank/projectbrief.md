# Project Brief
*Last Updated: 2026-04-16 20:15:00 IST*

## Project Overview
**Project Name**: Gauging Time Reversal Symmetry in Quantum Gravity: Arrow of Time from Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking
**Description**: A research paper proposing a mechanism for the cosmological arrow of time via spontaneous symmetry breaking of a local Z2 gauge field in Loop Quantum Gravity (LQG) using Tensor Network States (TNS).

## Objectives
1. Explain the origin of the arrow of time in a quantum gravity context.
2. Map Symmetry Protected Topological (SPT) phases to quantum geometry states.
3. Show how gauging time-reversal symmetry on spin networks leads to symmetry breaking and a preferred time direction.
4. Derive matter degrees of freedom (Dirac fermions) from gapless edge excitations of SPT phases.

## Key Features
- **Local Time Reversal**: Gauging Z2 symmetry on tensor networks.
- **LQG-TNS Correspondence**: Using spin networks as the substrate for tensor network states.
- **CZX Model Mapping**: Applying condensed matter models to 4-valent spin network vertices.
- **Tetrad Formalism**: Connecting microscopic symmetry breaking to the determinant of the tetrad field.
