## Key Results (Updated 2026-07-14)

- **Plaquette transition is SMOOTH** — no discontinuity or bimodality
- **Binder cumulant remains close to $2/3$** — no dramatic critical dip or first-order-style cumulant story is resolved
- **Peak locations drift upward with $L$** — reaching `β≈0.758` at `L=32`, consistent with a continuous-transition picture near the known 3D Ising-region value
- **Peak heights sharpen with $L$** — `χ_max` and `C_V,max` both increase from `L=16` to `L=32`
- **Simple peak-drift guide: $\beta_c(\infty) \approx 0.7618 \pm 0.0005$** — useful as consistency support, not as a precision claim
- **Critical exponents are deliberately not the headline result** — proof of principle is established; autocorrelation-aware precision extraction is deferred
