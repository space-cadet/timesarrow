## Key Results (2026-07-08)

- **Plaquette transition is SMOOTH** — no discontinuity or bimodality
- **Binder cumulant is FLAT** (~0.666) for L ≥ 16; critical drop to Ising ~0.47 not resolved
- **Peak shifts toward β ≈ 0.76** with increasing L (consistent with β_c ≈ 0.761)
- **β_c(∞) estimate: 0.750 ± 0.008** — close to literature but with large uncertainty
- **String tension decreases** approaching critical region (confinement → deconfinement)
- **Critical exponents NOT extracted** — data too coarse for reliable FSS
