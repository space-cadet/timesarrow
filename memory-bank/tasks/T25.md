## Interpretation Note

- Preserve the computed higher-valence signed-volume spectra.
- Treat $± q$ eigenvalue pairing as an **algebraic spectral reflection symmetry** (spectral symmetry of the operator), not by itself as proof of a physical $Z_2$ time-orientation symmetry.
- A physical $Z_2$ dynamical symmetry would require an explicit operator $	au$ acting on the relevant states that (i) commutes with the Hamiltonian, (ii) satisfies $	au^2 = 1$, and (iii) maps $+q$ eigenstates to $-q$ eigenstates. Constructing and testing such an operator is deferred to future work (T33 or later).
- The raw spectral data and computational results remain intact and valid; only the interpretation has been calibrated.
