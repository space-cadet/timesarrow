## Implementation Details
The EPRL vertex amplitude for a 4-valent vertex is:
```
A_v(j₁, j₂, j₃, j₄) = ∫_{SL(2,C)} dg₁...dg₄ δ(g₁g₂g₃g₄) ∏_{i=1}^4 D^{j_i}(g_i)
```
where D^{j} are SU(2) representation matrices and the integral is over SL(2,C).

**Key parameters:**
- Immirzi parameter γ = 0.237 (from black hole entropy matching)
- Boundary states: Livine-Speziale coherent states
- Face spins: j = 1/2, 1, 3/2, 2, 5/2, 3

**Implementation approach:**
1. Use the asymptotic formula from Donà et al. (2020/2022) for large j
2. For small j, compute the integral numerically using Monte Carlo or quadrature
3. Plot |A_v|² vs j for various vertex configurations

**Comparison with existing work:**
- Donà et al. (2020): "Numerical evaluation of spinfoam amplitudes" — their code/methods are the benchmark
- Donà et al. (2022): "Asymptotics of Lorentzian spinfoam amplitudes"
- Perez (2013): Review of spin foam approach

**Expected results:**
- Confirmation of A_v ~ j^(-α) with α > 0
- If α ≳ 2, j=1/2 dominance is strongly supported
- If α < 1, the dominance argument needs revision
- Model comparison: EPRL vs FK may show different α values
