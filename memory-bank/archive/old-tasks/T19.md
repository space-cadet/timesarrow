## Implementation Details
The pilot uses a hybrid Markdown-plus-raw-LaTeX approach. Section headings, prose, and lists live in Markdown, while equations, labels, `\autoref`, `\eqref`, citations, and manuscript-specific notation remain raw LaTeX. A small normalizer converts spaced inline math such as `$ Z_2 $` into Pandoc-friendly inline math before rendering. Standalone verification uses `article` + `biblatex`, so unresolved `??` references to the rest of the manuscript are expected until the generated section is inserted into the full SciPost document.
